B2B (calling businesses) → our scraper finds the leads. B2C (calling the general public) → scraping is off and the client uploads their own consented list, because individuals can't be scraped and AI-voice calls to consumers require their prior consent. Either way, every lead is normalized into the same format and flows through the same system below.
