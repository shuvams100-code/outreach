System Architecture

Updated 2026-07-02: outbound calling (cold-calling scraped or uploaded leads) was removed from the product — an AI voice call to someone who hasn't consented has no legal basis under U.S. phone calling law (the TCPA). This page now shows the real system: inbound-only, plus one narrow, consent-gated reminder feature. Nothing below can call someone who hasn't either called in themselves or said "yes" out loud on a live call.
The whole machine, end to end — from point zero (an organization is created), through the preset layer, the crew of specialist agents that answer every call, and out to every possible ending. Every cyan node is a frontend input — exactly what your onboarding screens must collect.
